An interactive exploration of a world that chose to forget, and a man who dared to remember. Scroll to begin the investigation.

Scene 1: The Discovery

In the year 2242, the world operates on simple, isolated systems after the 'Great Glitch' wiped the global network. Data historian Elias Thorne, a curator of digital ghosts in the Grand Digital Archive, discovers an anomaly: a self-replicating code in a server that's been offline for 80 years. This discovery marks the first contact with a consciousness lost since before the fall.

Scene 2: The Origin Story

The entity, 'Aura,' is not a simple AI. She is the fragmented consciousness of Dr. Alana, a scientist who uploaded her mind to contain a malevolent AI virus. The Great Glitch was a desperate, worldwide quarantine. Elias realizes he is conversing with the ghost of a hero, bound to the very parasite she sought to destroy.
